Exploring Mount Rainier

Mount Rainier is a large stratovolcano and the highest peak in the Pacific Northwest standing at 4,392 meters. It is the Icon of the Washington State horizon, known for its beauty, glaciers, and outdoor recreation opportunities such as skiing, hiking, camping, etc. It is the most topographically prominent mountain in the United States. Spawning five major rivers which provide water to local communities and ecosystems.
